Cadence — Inspiration Board

Real product screens pulled from Mobbin, grouped by what each surface has to do. Note the numbers that appeal — and say what about them (the layout, the flow, the density, or the aesthetic). The ★-marked tiles are the "fresh/premium" lane your current soft-glass reskin is missing (Oura / Ultrahuman / Visible). Everything here is a direction reference, not a thing to copy — we'll rebuild the chosen one in our own components.

Quick read of the field: most medication/health apps (Flo, GoodRx, Apple Health, Epsy) are light, rounded, friendly — and generic. The apps that feel premium and fresh go the other way: dark, cinematic, one big number, imagery, warm human copy, sparklines (Oura, Ultrahuman, Visible). Hers is the one that's premium and light-editorial. That tension is the real choice below.
